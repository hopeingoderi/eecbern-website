EEC Bern Website V47 – Church Premium Version

Quick update guide

1) Google Calendar auto updates
- Open: assets/js/config.js
- Paste your public Google Calendar embed URL into:
  googleCalendarEmbedUrl: "https://calendar.google.com/calendar/embed?src=..."

2) Sunday programme each week
- Edit: assets/data/sunday-programme.json
- Replace the poster image if needed:
  assets/img/sunday-programme-poster.svg
  or replace it with your own JPG/PNG and update the path in the JSON file.

3) Pastor profile
- Replace homepage preview image:
  assets/homev3/images/pastor-placeholder.svg

4) Sermons
- Open: assets/js/config.js
- Add your youtubeChannelId or youtubeFallbackVideoId
