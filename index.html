WELCOME

You are warmly welcome

We are a Christ‑centered Eritrean evangelical community in Bern. Our desire is simple: to worship Jesus, grow in the Word, and walk together as one family.

Whether you are new to faith, returning to church, or searching for hope — you are welcome to come as you are. We believe God restores, heals, and leads people into new life through Jesus Christ.

Psalm 122:1 “I rejoiced with those who said to me, ‘Let us go to the house of the LORD.’”
