Who We Are

A church home shaped by grace, truth, prayer and love.

We want every visitor to feel welcomed, seen and spiritually refreshed. Our desire is to be a peaceful home where people encounter Jesus, hear the Word of God, grow in holiness, and walk together in compassion and hope.

Love

Because God first loved us, we welcome people with humility, warmth and respect.

Faith

We trust Jesus Christ, stand on Scripture, and seek the guidance of the Holy Spirit.

Community

No one should walk alone. We carry one another in prayer, care and fellowship.

Statement of Faith

  • We believe the Bible is God's Word and our final authority.
  • We believe in God: Father, Son and Holy Spirit.
  • We believe Jesus Christ is Lord and Savior, fully God and fully man.
  • We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ.
  • We believe the Church is called to worship, disciple and serve.

For the weary heart

You do not have to carry everything alone.

Life can be heavy. Many people arrive carrying silent burdens, sorrow, confusion or spiritual hunger. Here we want them to discover the peace of Christ, the comfort of prayer, and the beauty of a church family that walks in love.
