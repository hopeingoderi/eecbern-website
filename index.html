WELCOME

A church family for worship, prayer and hope

We gather in Bern to worship Jesus, grow in God’s Word, and walk together as one family with faith, love, and hospitality.

Whether you are new to faith, returning to church, or searching for hope — you are welcome to come as you are. We believe God restores, heals, and leads people into new life through Jesus Christ.
