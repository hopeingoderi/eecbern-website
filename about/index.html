ABOUT

Who we are

Eritrean Evangelical Church Bern is a Christ‑centered community in Bern. We believe the Bible is God’s Word and Jesus Christ is the way, the truth, and the life.

John 14:6 “I am the way and the truth and the life…”
VISION

Life in Christ

To lead people to Jesus and walk together as one family in faith.
