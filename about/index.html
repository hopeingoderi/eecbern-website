ABOUT

Who we are

EEC Bern is a Christ-centered church family in Bern where worship, prayer, biblical teaching, and fellowship shape our life together. Our desire is simple: to worship Jesus, grow in the Word, and walk together as one family.

✦ Hebrews 10:25

“Let us not give up meeting together, but encourage one another.”

STATEMENT OF FAITH

✦We believe the Bible is God’s Word and our final authority.
✦We believe in one God: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
✦We believe Jesus Christ is Lord and Savior, fully God and fully man.
✦We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ.
✦We believe the church is called to worship, disciple, and serve.
✦We believe God still transforms lives, restores hope, and builds His people for His glory.
