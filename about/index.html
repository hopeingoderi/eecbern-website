About

Who we are

EEC Bern is a Christ‑centered community serving Eritrean believers and the wider community in Bern. We gather to worship, grow in discipleship, and live out the gospel together.

Vision

To know Christ

Growing in faith, holiness, and love — anchored in Scripture.

Mission

To make disciples

Serving the church, families, and the city with the gospel.

Values

Worship • Prayer • Community

Unity, compassionate service, and faithful teaching.
