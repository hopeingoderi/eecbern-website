About

Who we are

Eritrean Evangelical Church Bern (EEC Bern) is a Christ-centered community serving Eritrean believers and the wider community in Bern. We desire to glorify God through worship, discipleship, and mission.

Vision

To know Christ

Growing in faith, holiness, and love — anchored in Scripture.

Mission

To make disciples

Serving the church, families, and the city with the gospel.

Values

Worship • Prayer • Community

We commit to unity, generosity, and compassionate service.

Statement of faith (short)

  • We believe the Bible is God’s Word and our final authority.
  • We believe in one God: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • We believe Jesus Christ is Lord and Savior, fully God and fully man.
  • We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ.
  • We believe the church is called to worship, disciple, and serve.
