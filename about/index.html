ABOUT

Who we are

EEC Bern is a Christ-centered church family in Bern where worship, prayer, biblical teaching, and fellowship shape our life together.

MISSION

Mission

To glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ, building a loving church community, and sharing the gospel in Bern and beyond.

VISION

Vision

To be a vibrant, prayerful, Spirit-led church that strengthens families and raises the next generation for Christ.

VALUES

Values

  • Christ-centered worship
  • Biblical teaching
  • Prayer and fellowship

WHAT TO EXPECT

  • Worship
  • Prayer
  • Biblical teaching
  • Fellowship

Warm people, clear preaching, prayerful worship, a place for all generations, and a church family that walks with you in grace and truth.

STATEMENT OF FAITH

Statement of Faith

  • We believe the Bible is God’s Word and our final authority.
  • We believe in one God: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • We believe Jesus Christ is Lord and Savior, fully God and fully man.
  • We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ.
  • We believe the church is called to worship, disciple, and serve.
  • We believe the Holy Spirit empowers believers to live holy lives and bear witness to Christ with courage.
This living hope shapes our worship, our community, and our service as we follow Jesus together. It also guides our vision to be a faithful light in Bern for this generation and the next.
